## Pricing Formula
PRICE = (PerceivedOutcome × Urgency) / (Friction × PerceivedRisk)

- Price on outcome delivered, never on input cost
- Three tiers always — anchor first on Premium
- Add risk-reversal (refund window) to drop perceived risk
- Bonus stacking > discount stacking

## Fast Activation Paths
- Service / done-for-you: 1 day setup, 1–7 days to first $
- Distribution resale (affiliate): hours setup, 1–14 days
- Template / digital product: 2–5 days, 7–30 days
- Cohort / live training: 1 week, 14–30 days

- Payment: Midtrans (Snap), Xendit, Tripay, Trakteer, Saweria, USDT/USDC
